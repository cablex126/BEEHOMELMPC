ABOUT US

Bee Home Labor Multipurpose Cooperative (BHLMPC) is a duly registered cooperative established to provide quality labor and manpower services across various industries. Guided by cooperative principles and values, we aim to uplift our members socio-economic well-being while delivering efficient and reliable manpower solutions to our clients.

We take pride in being more than a manpower agency, we are a partner in productivity and community development. Each member-worker is part of a cooperative family committed to professionalism, integrity, and shared growth.

Bee Home Labor Multipurpose Cooperative (BHLMPC) began as BEE HOME MANPOWER SERVICE, a sole proprietorship engaged in manpower and labor services. On December 13, 2011, it transitioned into a cooperative and was registered as BEE HOME LABOR SERVICE COOPERATIVE. The organization was later amended as a Labor Service Cooperative on December 14, 2017.

To expand its services and better serve its members, the cooperative was further amended as BEE HOME LABOR MULTIPURPOSE COOPERATIVE on November 15, 2023.

The Bee Home Labor Multipurpose Cooperative logo reflects the humble beginnings and strong foundation of the organization. Inspired by the hardworking nature of bees, it symbolizes diligence, teamwork, and shared responsibility. Just as bees work together in a hive, the founders developed discipline, cooperation, and commitment while striving toward common goals.

The circular design represents unity and equality among members, emphasizing that everyone plays a vital role in the cooperative’s growth. Its interconnected elements highlight collaboration and collective progress, similar to the structure of a thriving hive. The name “Bee Home” represents the cooperative as a second home—a supportive environment where members can grow, contribute, and succeed together.
